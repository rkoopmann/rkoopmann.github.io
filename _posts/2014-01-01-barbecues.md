##INGREDIENTS

- 2 1/2 lbs hamburger
- 1 medium-diced onion
- 1 1/4 cups ketchup
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 1/4 cup brown sugar

##DIRECTIONS

1. Brown beef and onions
2. Bake 350 degrees hour
