## Ingredients

- 1 package Taco Seasoning
- 8 oz. sour cream
- 4 oz. salsa
- 4 oz. taco sauce
- 4 oz. cream cheese (softened)

## Instructions

- combine. spread. top with shredded lettuce, cheese, olives, etc.
