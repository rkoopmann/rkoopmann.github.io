## Ingredients

- 1 stick butter
- 12 oz chunk of velveta cheese
- 1 can mushroom soup
- 4 cups? shredded turkey (cooked)
- 12 sliced hamburger buns

## Instructions

- dump everything into a large crock pot on high heat. mix a few times throughout.
- serve on buns as sandwich.
