## Ingredients

- 1 lb Beef
- 2 Crescent tubes


## Instructions

1. Fry up taco beef as normal.
2. Arrange Crescent dough in circle.
3. Spoon taco meat onto dough and "fold" the dough over.
4. Top with cheddar cheese.
