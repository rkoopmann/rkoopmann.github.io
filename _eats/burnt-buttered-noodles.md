title: Burnt Buttered Noodles
time: 15 minutes
serves: 2
---


## Ingredients ##

- 3 cups elbow macaroni
- 1/2 stick butter

## Instructions ##

cook noodles. drain. set aside.

in same pan, place butter on med-high heat until it turns a tan color (burns).

add noodles. mix. serve.
